SERVICES

PREVENTATIVE MAINTENANCE

These are routine tasks designed to extend the life of the vehicle and ensure safety.


MINOR SERVICES:
Typically includes an engine oil and filter change, checking all fluid levels (coolant, brake fluid, power steering), and a basic safety inspection of lights and wipers. (Costs:R1,100-R2,500)
MAJOR SERVICES:
A deeper dive involving the replacement of spark plugs, air filters, fuel filters, and cabin filters. It often includes a full diagnostic scan and a thorough check of the braking system. (Costs:R2,200-R8,000+)
FLUID FLUSHES:
Over time, fluids degrade or become contaminated. Shops offer specialized flushes for the cooling system (antifreeze), transmission, and brake lines. (Costs:R1,000 to R3000+)

MECHANICAL REPAIRS

This covers the "heart" of the vehicle—the components that make it move.


ENGINE DIAGNOSTICS AND REPAIR:
Using OBD-II scanners to identify "Check Engine" light codes. Repairs can range from replacing sensors (like Oxygen or MAF sensors) to heavy mechanical work like head gasket replacements or engine overhauls. (Costs:R450-R1,00)
TRANSMISSION SERVICES:
Fixing gear-shifting issues, replacing clutches in manual cars, or servicing the torque converters and solenoids in automatic gearboxes. (Costs:R5,500-R12,000)
COOLING SYSTEM REPAIR:
Replacing leaking radiators, water pumps, thermostats, and cracked hoses to prevent engine overheating. (Costs:R1,500-R5,000+)

UNDERCARRIGE AND SAFETY SYSTEMS

These services focus on how the car handles and stops.


BRAKING SYSTEM:
Replacing worn brake pads and discs (rotors), surfacing drums, and repairing ABS (Anti-lock Braking System) sensors or master cylinders. (Costs:R1,800-R10,000+)
SUSPENSION AND STEERING:
Replacing shock absorbers, struts, control arms, and ball joints. This also includes power steering pump repairs to ensure the car handles smoothly. (Costs:R1,500-R5,000+)
WHEEL ALIGNMENT AND BALANCING:
Using specialized laser machinery to ensure the wheels are perfectly parallel and balanced, which prevents uneven tire wear and steering "pull." (Costs:R350-R1,000)

ELECTRICAL AND DIAGNOSTICS SERVICES

Modern cars are essentially computers on wheels, requiring specialized electronic expertise.


BATTERY AND ALTERNATOR:
Testing battery health, replacing faulty alternators (which charge the battery), and fixing starter motors. (Costs:R3,500-R9,000+)
AUTO-ELECTRICAL WIRING:
Tracing short circuits, fixing faulty power windows, central locking systems, and dashboard instrumentation. (Costs:R3,100-R5,500+)
AIR CONDITIONING:
Re-gassing the AC system with refrigerant, detecting leaks in the evaporator, and replacing the AC compressor. (Costs:R500-R1,500)

SPECIALIZED SERVICES

Some shops focus on specific niches to differentiate themselves we do them all.


DRIVETRAIN AND DIFF SERVICES
Repairing the differential and CV joints (Constant Velocity joints) which transfer power from the transmission to the wheels. (Costs:R2,00-R6,000+)
EXHAUST SYSTEMS
Patching leaks, replacing catalytic converters, or installing performance mufflers. (Costs:R1,500-R5,100+)
PRE-PURCHASE INSPECTIONS
A comprehensive "bumper-to-bumper" report provided to a potential buyer to identify hidden issues before they purchase a used vehicle. (Costs:R100-R2,000)
