## Project Overview

REVIVE GARAGE
Revive garage is a motor vehicle repair services company that has been repairing/bringing to life motor vehicles of customers for 5 years starting from 2021. This business was started by Mr Kole, born 1900, who in his time of living was not happy of the quality of services being served by garages, your car couldn’t even last for a full year without it being repaired again. Since 2021 it has been rated as best in quality of services to customers, but as the years go the business has been receiving less customers because the quality they serve keeps motor vehicles alive and strong for a long time meaning the possibility of a car coming back to be serviced will be after around 2 to 3 years minimum.


MISSION STATEMENT
“For Revive Garage to have a social footprint which will bring more customers to the business.”

VISSION SATEMENT
“To be able to have Revive Garage all around the world and for it to be ranked the number 1 motor vehicle service company.”


TARGET AUDIENCE
Revive Garage is targeting motor vehicle lovers who have their vehicles at home in need of some reviving for them to hit the road again back where they belong. 


## Website Goals and Objectives

THE WEBSITE GOALS AND OBJECTIVES THAT NEED TO BE ARCHIVED ARE:
•	Revive Grage is in need of a website to gain customers all over the world, because they only have a small loyal group of customers who are located around or near the business, this means the business is not known as much and it is not making any profit because of the small group of customers they have. A social media face is needed for the business because firstly it doesn’t have any and for the growth of the business so that it can be known worldwide for the quality of services the garage serves bringing more customers and the possibility of expansion around the world.

THE KEY PERFORMANCE INDICATORS ARE:
•	The Influx of customers/users of the website.
•	The be able to reach/attract foreign customers/users.
•	The Income being much greater than the expenses.
•	The possibility/ability of being able to open stores in different countries to deliver our services to customers all over the world.
