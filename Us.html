UNDERCARRIAGE AND SAFETY SYSTEMS

This category includes services related to the vehicle's suspension, steering, and braking systems, which are crucial for safe handling and stopping.


BRAKING SYSTEM:

Replacing worn brake pads and discs (rotors), surfacing drums, and repairing ABS (Anti-lock Braking System) sensors or master cylinders.
(Costs:R1,800-R10,000+)

SUSPENSION AND STEERING:

Replacing shock absorbers, struts, control arms, and ball joints. This also includes power steering pump repairs to ensure the car handles smoothly.
(Costs:R1,500-R5,000+)

WHEEL ALIGNMENT AND BALANCING:

Using specialized laser machinery to ensure the wheels are perfectly parallel and balanced, which prevents uneven tire wear and steering "pull."
(Costs:R350-R1,000)
