PREVENTIVE MAINTENANCE

ENGINE DIAGNOSTICS AND REPAIR:

Using OBD-II scanners to identify "Check Engine" light codes. Repairs can range from replacing sensors (like Oxygen or MAF sensors) to heavy mechanical work like head gasket replacements or engine overhauls.
(Costs:R450-R1,00)

TRANSMISSION SERVICES:

Fixing gear-shifting issues, replacing clutches in manual cars, or servicing the torque converters and solenoids in automatic gearboxes.
(Costs:R5,500-R12,000)

COOLING SYSTEM REPAIR:

Replacing leaking radiators, water pumps, thermostats, and cracked hoses to prevent engine overheating.
(Costs:R1,500-R5,000+)
